Coding Kickstart Camp, Session 1

Dates: June 15th - June 17th, 2016
Timing: 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Location: Dorseyville Middle School - Pittsburgh, PA
Description: Over this three-day coding "bootcamp," students with limited experience were introduced to the fundamental ideas of computer science and taught how to apply them in their code. Students began to learn the Java programming language and gained a thorough understanding of the language's basic syntax, style, and control structures. As their final project, they used Java to code their very own graphical calculator.
